# Mining Clicker - Incremental Game

An idle/clicker incremental game where you mine ores, hire dwarves, manage minecarts, and fight enemies to earn gold and prestige!

## Features

- **Clicking & Auto-Clickers**: Click the rock to mine gold, or purchase auto-clickers for passive income
- **Ore Mining**: Unlock and upgrade different ore types (Coal, Copper, Iron, Gold, Platinum, Emerald, Diamond)
- **Dwarves**: Hire dwarves to automatically mine ores for you
- **Minecarts**: Collect ores and deliver them for gold
- **Combat System**: Hire soldiers to defend against enemies and horde waves
- **Prestige System**: Reset your progress to gain permanent upgrades
- **Slot Machine**: Gamble for rewards and temporary multipliers

## How to Play

1. Click the central rock to mine gold
2. Purchase upgrades to increase your click power and efficiency
3. Unlock ores in the shop to start mining valuable resources
4. Hire dwarves and soldiers to automate your mining and defense
5. Prestige when you've earned enough gold to gain permanent bonuses
